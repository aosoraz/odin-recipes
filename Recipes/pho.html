Pho

Ingredients :

  • Marrow bones and Meaty bones
  • Thinly sliced raw meat
  • Yellow onions
  • Whole garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Whole spices: star anise, cloves, cinnamon sticks, black cardamom pods, fennel seeds, coriander seeds
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Dried noodles/Rice noodles

Steps :

  1. Pick a large enough bowl.
  2. Add your pre-cooked noodles
  3. Raw meat next
  4. Add the broth (must be super hot)
  5. Garnish it with Fresh mint, Sprigs of Thai basil, Fresh cilantro, Green onions (scallions), Sliced white or red onion, Fish sauce, or whatever you like
