Rawon (for 6 servings)

Ingredients :

  • 500gr beef
  • 1 knee beef
  • 3 lime leaf
  • 1 tablespoon of tamarin water
  • 2 stalks of green onion
  • 1 tablespoon of royco beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 3L of water
  • 5 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 8 red onion
  • 5 pieces of keluak(Indonesian traditional spices), smashed and soaked in hot water
  • 4 hazelnut grains, roasted
  • 1cm of turmeric, burned
  • Some cooked short bean sprouts

Steps :

  1. Boil the beef and knee beef until half soft. Cut the meat according to taste. set aside.
  2. Add the Royco Beef Broth and salt to the boiling water.
  3. Saut√© ground spices, galangal, lime leaves, and lemongrass until fragrant and cooked.
  4. Put the pieces of meat into the stir fry, cook until the spices are absorbed.
  5. Add the stir-fried meat into the broth. Cook over low heat until the meat is tender.
  6. Serve warm with short bean sprouts
