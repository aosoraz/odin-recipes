Soto

Ingredients :

  • 1/2 free-range chicken, cut in half
  • 1.5 liters of water
  • 3 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, bruised
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 pieces of lime leaves
  • 5 grains of garlic
  • 3 shallots
  • 4 candlenuts
  • 3 cm turmeric
  • 1 cm ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated pepper
  • 2 teaspoon salt

Extra Ingredients (if you want to add) :

  • 100 g suun, brew hot water
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons spring onions
  • 4 tablespoon fried shallots

Steps :

  1. Bring the water to a boil, boil the chicken over low heat until the chicken is almost soft.
  2. Saut√© ground spices with lime leaves, bay leaves, lemongrass and stir until cooked and fragrant. Remove.
  3. Put it in the chicken stew. Boil over low heat until the chicken is soft.
  4. Remove the chicken, drain until slightly dry.
  5. Fry the chicken briefly until the outside is dry. Drain well.
  6. Coarse shredded chicken.
  7. Arrange the chicken, suun, bean sprouts in a serving bowl.
  8. Pour in hot broth.
  9. Serve with green onions, fried onions, chili sauce and lime.
